Help

Switching screens

Tap the menu (☰) top-right to flip between Converter and Board Feet & List.

Converter

Type a decimal inch (like 6.425) or a millimeter value. The other unit fills in automatically, and you get the nearest fraction above (cut long) and below (stop short) using tape-measure denominators: 1/2 down to 1/32.

Fraction → Decimal

Type a fraction across the three boxes — whole number, numerator, denominator. Leave the whole blank for fractions under an inch. The decimal and mm fields at the top update automatically.

Example: leave whole blank, type 9 and 32 to get 0.28125".

Board Feet

Enter thickness, width, and length to calculate board footage. Add a quantity for multiple identical pieces. Enter a price per board foot to see total cost.

Board feet formula: T × W × L ÷ 12

Cut List

Fill in the board feet fields, then tap + Add to Cut List. Each piece shows its footage and cost. The total at the bottom adds everything up. Tap ✕ on a row to remove it. Clear all wipes the whole list.

The list resets when you close the app.
